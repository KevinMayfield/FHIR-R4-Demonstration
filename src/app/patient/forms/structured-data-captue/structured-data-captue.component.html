Patient Forms Structured Data Capture Form [Automatic population](https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/sdc/populate.html) and [Extaction](https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/sdc/extraction.html) of Obsevations is at early stages of development (see also [US National Library of Medicine SDC Questionnaire App](https://lhcforms.nlm.nih.gov/lforms-fhir-app/?server=https://3cdzg7kbj4.execute-api.eu-west-2.amazonaws.com/poc/events/FHIR/R4)).
